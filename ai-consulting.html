Frequently asked questions

What can an AI agent actually do for my business?

It answers customer inquiries instantly at any hour, books appointments, guides customers to payment, qualifies leads and hands complex cases to your team with the full conversation attached. Most clients see the majority of routine inquiries resolved without human input.

Does it work on WhatsApp?

Yes. We deploy on the WhatsApp Business API with verified numbers and approved message templates, as well as on your website. One agent can serve both channels.

Will it answer in Arabic?

Yes. Our agents work in Arabic, English and French, and follow the language the customer writes in.

How do we start?

With a proof of concept: a working assistant trained on your own website content that you test before committing to a full rollout. Ask the assistant on this page for a demo.

How much does it cost?

It depends on channels, integrations and volume. Pricing is scoped after a short discovery conversation, and the proof of concept comes first.
