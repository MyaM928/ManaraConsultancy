Frequently asked questions

Do you work with pre-revenue startups?

Yes. Much of our work is at idea and pre-seed stage: validating the concept, writing the plan and preparing the first pitch.

Can you build my pitch deck?

Yes. We build the narrative, the deck and the financial story together, then rehearse you for investor questions.

Do you know the MENA market?

Yes. We are rooted in Lebanon with more than ten years across MENA, the Gulf and international markets, and we advise on market entry in both directions.

How long does a business plan take?

Typically two to four weeks depending on research depth and financial complexity. Deadlines for competitions or investors can be accommodated.
